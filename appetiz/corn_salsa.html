Corn Salsa

(serving size: ??)

Ingredients

  • 2 ears of sweet corn
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 1/2 pounds of tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 jalapeno, diced
  • 1 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons minced parsley
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice (1 lime)

Place corn in boiling water for 3 minutes, rinse under cold water and allow to drain.

Place tomatoes in a wire colander and set over a boil. Sprinkle with salt and toss to combine. Allow to sit 30 minutes to remove excess fluid. Blot with paper towel to remove fluid.

Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and chill overnight
