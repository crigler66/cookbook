Banana Bread

(serving size: 12)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 - 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1/3 cups sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preheat oven to 350(F)/177(C). Grease and flour the botton and sides of a loaf pan. Set aside.

Whisk together flour, baking soda and salt in a medium bow.
Mash bananas in a large bowl and whisk in sugar, eggs, melted butter, sour cream and vanilla.

Add flour mixture and stir gently until combined. Transfer to greased loaf pan and use spatula to smooth the top.
Bake for 35 - 40 minutes, until an inserted toothpick comes out clean or with a few moist crumbs. If bread starts to get too brown, tent with foil for the final 10 minutes.

Let bread cool on cooling rack in loaf pan for 15 - 20 minutes. Invert the loaf pan and carefully remove banana bread. Let bread continue to cool on the rack for about an hour. Slice and serve.
