Rosemary Focaccia

(serving size: small batch)

Ingredients

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast (1 .25 oz. package)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 1/3 cups warm water
  • 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried rosemary

Preheat oven to 375(F)/190(C) degrees.
[PROOF YEAST: In a medium bowl dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.]

Mix in salt, oil, 2 cups of bread flour and rosemary. Stir and then add last cup of flour. Mix dough until pliable.

Knead dough on floured surface until flat.

Place dough in oiled bowl, cover and let rise until it doubles in size (about 45 minutes).

Coat baking sheet with oil and gently press the dough out until about 1/2 inch thick.

Brush more oil over the top of the dough. Make dimples in the dough and sprinkle with parmesan cheese

Let rise again until it it doubles in size (about 20 minutes).

Bake for 25 minutes
