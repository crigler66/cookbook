Homemade Bread

(serving size: 2 loaves)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups warm water
  • 6 cups bread flour
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Preheat oven to 350(F)/177(C). Grease the botton and sides of 2 loaf pans. Set aside.

Mix warm water, yeast and sugar in bowl and let sit for 5 minutes (proof). Mix in salt and oil, then mix in flour one cup at a time until fully mixed and sooth. Leave in bowl and cover with a damp towel for 1 1/2 hours to rise. Divide in equal halves and place each half in a greased pan. Let rise another 1/2 hour in the pan.
Bake for 30 minutes for loaves, 20 minutes if made into rolls.
Let cool for at least 1 hour before slicing.
