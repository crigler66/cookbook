Operating Instructions

Prepare to Cook

The air fryer's high-speed fans cook your favorite foods quickly. The rubber feet on the racks elevate the racks to allow air to circulate around foods for even cooking and crisping. The racks also allow excess fat to drip down and away from foods for healthier eating.

CAUTION: Nevre fill the basket with oil. Unlike deep fryer, air fryers require little to no oil to produce a crisp texture. If you choose to use oil, toss foods with just a little in a separate bowl before cooking in the air fryer.

  1. Prepare the food(s) you plan to air fry. (See the Timing Chart for how to prepare and air fry some favorite foods.)
  2. Place the air fryer on a hard flat surface and plut it in. The control panel will illuminate in white. Press the "1" button to activate basket 1 so it is ready to have its cooking time and temperature set. Alternatively, press the "2" button to activate basket 2.
