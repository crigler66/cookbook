Banana Pudding Cookies

(serving size: 24 cookies)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 sticks of butter
  • 3/4 cups light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 3.4(oz) instant banana cream pudding dry mix only
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 1/4 cups white chocolate chips

Preheat oven to 350(F)177(c)

Combine the flour and baking soda in a medium mixing bowl and set aside.
In a separate large mixing bowl cream your butter and sugars together using a hand mixer.
Add in the dry pudding mix and continue to beat with the hand mixer until well blended.

Add your eggs and vanilla and beat with the hand mixer untill smooth and no lumps remain.
Combine your wet and dry mixtures, stir together with a wooden spoon. Add the white chocolate chips and gently fold together.
Roll into 1-inch balls and place on a parchment paper lined baking sheet.

Bake these cookies at 350(F)/177(C) for 8 - 9 minutes. You want to take them out of the oven before they start to brown.
Eat warm or let cool to enjoy!
