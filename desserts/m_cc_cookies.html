Mom's Chocolate Chip Cookies

(serving size: ??)

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 3/4 sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 large egg
  • 1 package chocolate chips
  • 1 cup butter, softened

Preheat oven to 375(F)/190(C). In a mixer or bowl combine all the dry ingredients. In a separate bowl blend the eggs, butter and vanilla

Add wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix well. Add in chocolate chips.
Drop rounded teaspoons of mixed dough onto an ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake for 8 minutes
