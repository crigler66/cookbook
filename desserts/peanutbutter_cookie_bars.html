Peanut Butter Cookie Bars

(serving size: 16 - 24 bars)

Ingredients

    For the cookie bars
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups light brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
    For the chocolate frosting
  • 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened coco powder
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 tablespoons water

For the cookie bars
Preheat oven to 350(F)/177(C).

Cream butter, brown sugar and peanut butter together until creamy. Add in eggs, vanilla, and salt. Combine flour and baking powder with dough and continue mixing for another 30 seconds, dry ingredients are mixed in with the dough. Using a rubber spatula, spread the dough mixture into a lightly sprayed 13 x 9 inch baking pan

Bake for 18 - 20 minutes, or until the dough sets (It will begin to turn golden brown).

Remove from the oven and allow to cool before frosting.
