Sugar Cookies

(serving size: 5 1/2 dozen)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup white sugar for decoration

Preheat oven to 350(F)/175(C) degrees. In a large bowl, cream together the shortening and 1 1/2 cups of sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Combine the flour, baking powder and salt; stir into the creamed mixture until well blended. Cover dough and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Roll dough into 1 inch balls and roll the balls into the remaining sugar. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake for 8 to 9 minutes or until barely golden. Allow cookies to cool for at least 2 minutes before removing from cookie sheet.
