Blondies

(serving size: 16 bars)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup butterscotch or peanutbutter chips

Preheat oven to 350(F)177(c). Lightly grease and flour a 8x8-inch pan.

Wisk together the melted butter and sugar in a bowl. Add the egg and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Then mix in the butterscotch/peanutbutter chips.
Pour into the pan and spread evenly. Bake for 18 to 25 minutes or until tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
Allow to cool. Cut into squares and serve.
NOTE: Blondies can be stored in an air tight container at room temperature for up to 1 week or frozen for up to 3 months.
