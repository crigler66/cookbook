Banana Cream Cheescake Bars

(serving size: 9 bars)

Ingredients

  • 1 (11 oz.) box of Nilla Wafer, crushed into crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 (8 oz.) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (3.4 oz.) package instant banana cream pudding mix
  • 1 bananas, sliced (optional)
  • 1 container of Cool Whip (optional)

Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper. Combine the Nilla Wafer crumbs and melted butter in a medium bowl. Press mixture into prepared baking dish and place into refrigerator to chill.

In a chilled bowl, using a handheld mixer, whip heavy cream and sugar until stiff peaks form and set aside. In a another large bowl, beat cream cheese untill smooth. Add pudding mix and beat until combined. Fold in whipped cream until smooth.

Pour combined filling mixture evenly into the chilled crust. Cover and refrigerate for at least 8 hours before cutting into 9 squares and garnishing with Cool Whip and banana slices.
