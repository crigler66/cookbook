Apple Pie Moonshine

(serving size: yields 2 1/2 gallons)

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon apple juice
  • 1 gallon apple
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 4 cinnamon sticks
  • 1 pinch apple pie spice
  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle of 190 proof grain alcohol (Everclear)
  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle of vodka

Combine apple juice, apple cider, sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon sticks, and apple pie spice in a large pot; bring to boil.
Cover the pot with a lid, reduce heat and simmer for about an hour.
Remove from heat and let cool completely.

Stir grain alcohol and vodka into apple mixure and remove cinnamon sticks.
Pour apple pie moonshine into clean glass jars or bottles and store in the refrigerator.
