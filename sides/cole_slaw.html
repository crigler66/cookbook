Cole Slaw

(serving size: 8 - 10)

Ingredients

  • 2 bags of shredded slaw cabbage
  • 1 cup Miracle Whip
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup onion chopped

Combine all ingredients in a large bowl and serve or cover and chill in refrigerator until time to serve.
