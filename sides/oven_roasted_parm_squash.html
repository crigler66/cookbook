Oven Roasted Parmesan Squash and Zucchini

(serving size: 6)

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchini, small
  • 2 yellow squash, small
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely minced or 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • fresh cracked black pepper, to taste

Preheat oven to 350(F)/175(C). In a medium bowl mix together garlic and oil. Set aside.
Cut squash and zucchini into half inch thick slices and toss the squash and zucchini in the mixture.

Line the slices up in a sheet pan and cover with parmesan cheese.
Bake in over for 30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Remove from over and season with fresh cracked black pepper and serve.
